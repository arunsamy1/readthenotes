💡 How to Choose an ETF

Your choice depends on your investment strategy and view of the semiconductor industry.

  • For maximum exposure to the largest companies: SMH is highly concentrated in top-tier firms like NVIDIA, with its top 10 holdings making up over 75% of the fund . This offers high potential returns but with greater risk.
  • For a diversified approach: SOXX provides a more balanced portfolio by capping the weight of its largest holdings, reducing the influence of any single company .
  • For broad sector coverage with an equal-weight strategy: XSD holds a larger number of stocks and weights them more evenly, which can reduce risk and provide exposure to smaller, specialized semiconductor companies .
